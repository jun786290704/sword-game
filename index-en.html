physical strength

Each character has its own physical pool, up to 200 points. Recover a little every five minutes. Physical strength is consumed when the player participates in the battle. Each battle consumes 40 / 80 / 120 / 160 / 200 physical strength, which depends on the physical strength consumption of each battle selected by the player. This means that within 24 hours, each character can gain 288 physical strength, equivalent to 7.2 battles.

How it works

Swords is a meta universe game on the binance smart chain. Anyone can use their valuable NFT assets to fight the enemy and earn FCHS tokens.

Whitelist

Follow the Tasks to be eligible on Whitelist Spot, buy FCHS and become early Adventurers.

Get Rewarded

Earn FCHS tokens on every won fights.

Discover NFT

By acquiring FCHS you can buy NFT Adventurers from SWords NFT marketplace.

Weapon battle

Heroes must obtain weapons from the blacksmith shop in order to reach the underground world. They must be protected by weapons so that they will not be killed by those enemies and destroy their creatures in order to live in the world of swords..

Fight

To start the game and win the journey, you must have at least 1 hero and 1 weapon to fight.
